Hi, I'm Tralyn.

E-mail | Github | Linked In | Instagram

I'm 22 years old currently a senior computer science student at the University of Nevada-Las Vegas. I'm a programmer and 3D artist. I currently work at 60 East Technologies as a member of technical staff intern. I develop QA tests in Python for the program and am in charge of code coverage.

Constantly craving a challenge, I'm always trying to learn new things. I'm interested in software development, web development, app development, game development, 3D modeling, virtual reality, cybersecurity, and more. I've been making websites since I was 11 years old and even 11 years later nothing has changed.

I am a hobbyist 3D artist. I love creating 3D models then bringing them to life with code. What does that entail exactly? Well, I create my own 3D models, texture them, then bring them into engines such as Unity or Unreal. From there I write code to interact with my objects. My dad likes to say that I know how to build a car and make it drive.

I primarily use Autodesk Maya, Autodesk 3dsMax, Substance Painter, Substance Designer, Pixologic Zbrush, and Adobe Photoshop. I focus my 3D work on game assets and architecture studies, specifically hard surface modeling. However, I'm not a stranger to digital sculpting either.

I've attended the University of California-Riverside's Game Development Program and received a certificate of completion for the program and was rewarded hardest working student. We specifically focused on learning Autodesk Maya and coding in Unity with C#. I've also taken an online digital sculpting course from MoldAcademy instructed by Mike Defeo, the character artist for works such as Despicable Me and Ice Age.
